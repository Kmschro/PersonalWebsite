Personal Bio

My name is Klaus Schroeder and I am a driven individual currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Science at Quinnipiac University, Hamden, Connecticut, with an expected graduation in May 2025. I hold an Associate degree in web development from Norwalk Community College, where I graduated Cum Laude in 2022. Prior to that, I completed my High School Diploma at P-TECH High School, Norwalk, Connecticut, graduating with a 4.1 GPA and membership in the National Honor Society.

My professional journey includes valuable experiences such as my role as a Retail/Pharmacy Associate at CVS from June 2023 to August 2023. During this time, I prioritized positive customer experiences through attentive service, maintained accurate cash transactions, and ensured a well-organized and visually appealing store environment.

I also had the opportunity to intern at IBM, where I worked in the Energy Resources Division in the summer of 2022. My responsibilities included conducting interviews with renewable energy stakeholders, contributing to the design of a user interface for energy monitoring, and delivering a final presentation outlining our project objectives and solutions. Additionally, I contributed significantly to the IBM Cloud and Cognitive Division in the summer of 2021, where my team's innovative use of chatbots earned us 1st place in the IBM Internship Expo.

My skill set includes proficiency in HTML, CSS, Java, JavaScript, Git, GitHub, and PHP, alongside strong familiarity with Microsoft Office and Google Suite. I possess excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, and thrive in collaborative, goal-oriented environments. Additionally, my ability to remain composed under pressure enables me to effectively navigate challenging situations.

I am eager to leverage my skills and experiences to contribute meaningfully to dynamic and innovative teams, and I am excited to explore new opportunities in the field of computer science and web development.
